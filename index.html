About the Movement

The Iconic
Movement

JUNUB GOT TALENT is South Sudan's premier creative talent platform — built to discover, develop, and elevate the most gifted artists, athletes, and creatives across all 10 states of South Sudan and beyond.

Founded in 2025 by ICON DEGREAT, JGT is more than a talent show. It is a movement — a declaration that South Sudanese creativity belongs on the world stage.

"They didn't give me a seat — so I built the throne."

— ICON DEGREAT, CEO & Founder

What We Do

Programs & Initiatives

01
Talent Discovery

Nationwide scouting across all 10 states. We find raw diamonds in cities, towns, and villages — and bring them into the light.

02
Studio Development

Artists gain access to professional studio sessions, production mentorship, and partnerships with established producers. Real music, real quality.

03
Global Distribution

We connect South Sudanese talent to international streaming platforms, media networks, and global audiences. Juba to the world.

04
Artist ID & Certification

Official JGT Artist Identification certifies every member. Carry the mark of Originality, Professionalism, and Unity.

05
JGT Academy Coming 2027

A full creative academy offering education in music, business law, global branding, and entrepreneurship for South Sudan's next generation.

06
Partnerships & Investment

Sponsorship models and investor frameworks connecting the East African creative economy to South Sudan's most promising talent ecosystem.

The Founder

Born to Build
Something Iconic

Luka Mathuc Abiem — known as ICON DEGREAT — is a 21-year-old digital creator, graphic designer, video director, and entrepreneur from Warrap State, South Sudan. Fluent in Dinka, Arabic, English, Luganda, and Swahili.

He founded Junub Got Talent at just 19, becoming one of the youngest CEOs in South Sudan's creative sector. His music genre — self-coined as "Victory" — fuses traditional South Sudanese rhythms with Afrobeats and Hip-Hop.

2019 Enrolled at Juba Private Secondary School. Began independent learning in design and digital creation.
2023 Completed secondary education. Launched music career at Wekou Studio with Slatine Pro. Joined JKM Service Group as Graphic Designer.
2024 Founded JUNUB GOT TALENT at age 19. Built and launched the JGT mobile app. Published the JGT Brand Manifesto.
2026 Issued official JGT CEO credentials. Expanding platform across all 10 states. Preparing JGT Season 2026 — Call for Icons is open.
